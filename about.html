ABOUT US

This is Takara Sushi - the most delicious sushi in suburban Minneapolis. Ever since 2015, we strived to create the best sushi restaurant possible. Only the freshest ingredients create the most delectable dishes, and we do this not only for the sushi, but for everything else to complement the experience - cutlets, udon, shrimp, rolls, and more!

So what are you waiting for? Stop by at Takara Sushi for the best sushi you'll ever have in Minnesota!
